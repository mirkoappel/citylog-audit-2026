About the Hotel

Harbour Plaza Amsterdam occupies a row of lovingly restored 17th-century merchant houses on the Prinsengracht, one of Amsterdam's iconic ring canals. The building's original timber beams and sandstone facades have been preserved; inside, forty-two individually designed rooms blend warm Dutch craftsmanship with contemporary comfort.

Guests are a short walk from the Anne Frank House, the Westerkerk, and the galleries of the Negen Straatjes shopping quarter. The Centraal Station is twelve minutes by tram, and Schiphol Airport is reachable in under thirty minutes by direct rail.

Dining — De Sluiswachter Brasserie

Our ground-floor brasserie opens directly onto a private canal terrace. Head Chef Lotte Vermeer composes a menu anchored in Dutch seasonal produce — North Sea sole, Zeeland mussels, and aged Gouda sourced from the Saturday market on Noordermarkt. The wine list highlights small-production Burgundy and Alsatian Rieslings selected by our in-house sommelier.
